## Advantages
- Decentralized protocol (Matrix)
- Default end-to-end encryption
- Self-hostable for full data sovereignty
- Federated communication across servers

## Disadvantages
- Less polished UX than Slack
- Smaller integration marketplace
- Server administration overhead if self-hosting

## Migration Difficulty
**Medium** — Channel structure maps directly, but Slack-specific apps and workflows must be replaced with Matrix bots and bridges. Users need to adapt to a federated identity model.
